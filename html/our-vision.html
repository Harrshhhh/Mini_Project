OUR CORE VALUES

SJCEM Canteen in your pocket

The vision for a college canteen should encompass creating a welcoming and comfortable environment where students can relish healthy, affordable, and diverse food options that cater to their dietary preferences. Our goal should be to promote a sense of community by providing a social atmosphere that encourages students to connect and engage with one another. Furthermore, we strive to ensure sustainability by using eco-friendly packaging, reducing food waste, and sourcing locally grown ingredients. Our unwavering commitment to hygiene and cleanliness ensures the safety and well-being of our customers. We envision a college canteen that is all-inclusive, promoting a positive environment that enhances the overall college experience for our students.
